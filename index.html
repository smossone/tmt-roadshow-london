Key Takeaways

Germany dominates on revenue and scale (INFORM, Seeburger, Raisin, Senacor all above $150M revenue), while the Baltics offer the highest growth/value arbitrage (Oxylabs, Gurtam, Zabbix, Paysera — fast-growing with lower entry valuations).

B2B software is the sector with broadest investor overlap — 12+ investors target this space. INFORM, Seeburger, Oxylabs, Gurtam, and Zabbix all sit in this sweet spot.

Poland is underrepresented in investable, independent targets at scale. Kamsoft is the standout; many Polish tech companies have already been acquired by PE or are subsidiaries.

Baltic fintech (Paysera) offers strong targets for debt-focused investors (Viola Credit, Ashgrove, CLP) who can provide growth capital without dilution.
